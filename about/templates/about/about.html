A cultivation with genuine respect for living things. A cultivation where balance is more important than maximum yield. Without the use of easily soluble artificial fertilizers and chemical pesticides and growth promoters. And we do what we can to find seed producers around the world who share the same attitude. The standard varieties, those that are normally self-pollinating or open-pollinated, will continue to dominate our assortment. Not for nostalgic or conservative reasons, but because many of them give excellent yields in several aspects through their own power. They are too good to disappear forever. Many of them hang by a thin thread, and together we can contribute to saving some from oblivion.
